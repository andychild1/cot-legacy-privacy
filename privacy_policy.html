Privacy Policy – COT LEGACY

Developer: Andrea Tosetto Version: 1.0
Last updated: 17-08-2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the COT LEGACY mobile application (the “App”) handles information. The App is designed for market analysis using public datasets and does not collect personal data from users.

2. Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data such as names, emails, phone numbers, contacts, or precise location information.

3. Data Sources Used by the App

The App processes only public, market-related data, including:

  • Commitments of Traders (COT) reports published by the U.S. Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC).
  • Historical price data retrieved from publicly available sources such as Yahoo Finance.

These datasets are not linked to any individual user and are used solely for display and analysis within the App.

4. Advertising and In‑App Purchases

If the App displays advertising (e.g., via Google AdMob), Google may process data in accordance with its own policies to deliver and measure ads. If the App offers in‑app purchases or subscriptions, transactions are handled securely by Google Play; the App does not access or store payment information.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • About Google AdMob & data

5. Permissions

The App is designed to work without requesting sensitive device permissions. Network access is used solely to download public market data and to serve ads (if enabled).

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data processed by the App. Because the App does not collect personal data, there is no user profile or account to secure within the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Your continued use of the App after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
